# Comfort Corner

Welcome to **Comfort Corner** — your personal digital safe space! This is a cozy sanctuary where you can track tiny daily wins, plant virtual garden flowers, let go of heavy thoughts, and get gentle validation. 

You'll be accompanied by **Barnaby**, a very wholesome and fluffy digital panda who is always proud of you!

---

### Features
* **Tiny Wins**: Celebrate your small, daily accomplishments.
* **Mood Garden**: Watch your sanctuary bloom as you log your feelings.
* **Let It Go**: Write down heavy thoughts and release them into the breeze.
* **Panda Wisdom**: Heartwarming, personalized messages powered by Gemini.

---
*Remember: You are doing great, and Barnaby believes in you! *
